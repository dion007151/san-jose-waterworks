Water Treatment Process

Our advanced 6-step treatment process:

  1. Intake & Screening - River water collection
  2. Coagulation & Flocculation - Particle removal
  3. Sedimentation - Settling of particles
  4. Filtration - Multi-layer filtration
  5. Disinfection - Chlorination & UV treatment
  6. Storage & Distribution - Clean water storage
